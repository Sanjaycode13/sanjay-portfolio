Key features include:

  • Interactive filters for time, region, and trend types
  • Map visuals, KPI indicators, and comparison panels
  • Linked views with drill-down analytics
This dashboard reduced analysis time by 40% and helped non-technical stakeholders make faster, data-informed decisions during pandemic response planning.
