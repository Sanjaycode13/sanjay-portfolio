SimuMed – Surgical Education Redefined (In Progress)

  • Tech Stack: Unity, C#, Blender, OpenXR, DICOM

Overview: SimuMed is an MR-based surgical training platform designed to minimize reliance on cadavers and elevate training realism. It includes high-fidelity 3D anatomy, bleeding simulation, and haptic interaction.

Objectives: Improve retention, reduce ethical/logistical issues in medical education, and offer interactive skill-building scenarios through MR.

Implementation: Using Unity and OpenXR, users can perform procedures on lifelike 3D models created via Blender and DICOM imaging data. Real-time feedback and timeline-based guided training are implemented.

Expected Outcome: 80% reduction in cadaver usage, 60% increase in retention, scalable training model for global medical education institutions.
